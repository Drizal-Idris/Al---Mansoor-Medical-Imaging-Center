Introduction

The Institution was started in 2009 as family business and purpose was to serve the Mumias community and its environs, later in the day it was made a non profit making organisation under Spreading Smiles initative.

We now have an operational out patient facility with several diagnostic departments.

We have qualified & Registered staff accross several available cadres within the facility.

We have 15 proffesionals and four casual staff.

We are located in Kakamega county, Mumias Sub County, Lukoye Centre paradise Road.

Approximately 1km from Mumias Town, off Mumias - Bungoma Road and 200 metres Paradise - Shitukhumi tarmac Road.
